Shortcuts and tips

Alt+Shift+B toggles the click-to-blur picker. Alt+Shift+H is the panic blur that hides the whole page. Change both at chrome://extensions/shortcuts. Click-to-blur, area blurs, and the tab title mask persist per site and re-apply when you come back.

RosterBlur helps you protect student information while screen sharing. It is a visual privacy tool, not a compliance product, and does not make you or your school FERPA compliant on its own.
