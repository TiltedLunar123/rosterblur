Thanks for going Pro!

RosterBlur Pro is a lifetime license. Your key is issued below; it is verified on your device and never phones home.

Fetching your license key...

  1. Right-click the RosterBlur icon and pick Options.
  2. Under Pro License, paste the key and click Activate.
  3. Add your class rosters and roster blur takes it from there.
